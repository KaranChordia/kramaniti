# Content Calendar

**Purpose:** Maintain Kramaniti's planned content across channels, pillars, campaigns, drafts, approvals, and publishing status.

**Key Findings:** [Recommendation] The content calendar should remain strategy-led and approval-gated. It should not become a volume tracker.

**Evidence:** `04_content_system/content_pillars.md`, `03_brand_strategy/brand_system/brand_operating_kit.md`, and `09_reviews/proof_register.md`.

**Interpretation:** [Inference] A small, consistent cadence is better for Kramaniti than a large calendar of generic AI posts.

**Implications:** Every planned item should show its content pillar, channel, owner, proof status, and approval state before publishing.

**Open Questions:** Which channels are active first? What is the founder's realistic review cadence?

**Next Steps:** Fill this calendar only after a campaign or monthly theme is approved.

---

## Calendar Rules

- Use content pillars from `04_content_system/content_pillars.md`.
- Use campaign briefs from `04_content_system/campaign_briefs.md`.
- Route public proof claims through `09_reviews/proof_register.md`.
- Do not mark an item as approved unless the founder has approved it.
