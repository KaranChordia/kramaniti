# Campaign Briefs

**Purpose:** Store campaign plans that connect Kramaniti's positioning, content, website direction, distribution, and proof review.

**Key Findings:** [Recommendation] Campaigns should start from a business goal and audience problem, not from a channel or trend.

**Evidence:** `03_brand_strategy/brand_system/brand_operating_kit.md`, `04_content_system/pillars/content_pillars.md`, and `06_ai_agent_context/system_prompts/master_context.md`.

**Interpretation:** [Inference] Kramaniti campaigns should teach the method and lead toward qualified conversations rather than chase broad reach.

**Implications:** A campaign brief must define the strategic angle, audience, content formats, website support, distribution plan, proof risks, and review path.

**Open Questions:** Which offer should the first campaign support? Which channel should be primary?

**Next Steps:** Copy the brief template below for each approved campaign.
