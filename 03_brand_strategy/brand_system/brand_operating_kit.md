# Brand Operating Kit

**Purpose:** Give the Digital Presence Orchestrator one practical brand kit for Kramaniti's public presence across website, content, campaigns, decks, and assets.

**Key Findings:** [Fact] Kramaniti's public presence must follow the sequence: strategy before tools, systems before scale, content after clarity. [Recommendation] Brand work should be managed as one connected growth system instead of separate design, copy, and content tasks.

**Evidence:** `06_ai_agent_context/system_prompts/master_context.md`, `03_brand_strategy/positioning/brand_identity_guidelines.md`, `03_brand_strategy/positioning/positioning_analysis.md`, `03_brand_strategy/brand_narrative.md`, `04_content_system/content_pillars.md`, and `09_reviews/decisions.md`.

**Interpretation:** [Inference] Kramaniti needs the operating discipline of an early brand team: strategy, identity, content, website, distribution, assets, and proof review moving through one governed workflow.

**Implications:** The Digital Presence Orchestrator owns the overall system. Specialist agents produce inputs, but public claims, publishing, pricing, client references, and major positioning changes still require founder approval.

**Open Questions:** Which public channels are approved as active channels first? Which assets are approved for repeated use in social content, decks, and proposals?

**Next Steps:** Use this kit before creating campaigns, calendar plans, website copy, public assets, or distribution reports.

---

## 1. Operating Principle

[Fact] Kramaniti is not an AI automation agency. Kramaniti is a first-principles AI systems partner that connects strategy, operational infrastructure, and cinematic content into one brand growth pipeline.

Every digital presence task must pass this sequence:

1. Strategy before tools.
2. Systems before scale.
3. Content after clarity.

## 2. Digital Presence Team

| Function | Lead Agent | Core Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Orchestration | Digital Presence Orchestrator | Scope goals, select sub-agents, assemble final plan, enforce approvals |
| Brand identity | Brand Identity Agent | Keep visual rules, brand kit, logo use, color, typography, and design consistency aligned |
| Brand strategy | Brand Strategist | Maintain positioning, audience clarity, offer framing, and campaign angles |
| Content direction | Content Director | Own pillars, themes, post ideas, scripts, newsletters, and editorial calendar |
| Narrative editing | Narrative Editor | Turn strategy into polished public copy and founder-led language |
| Website stewardship | Website Steward | Review website messaging, CTA flow, page structure, and conversion clarity |
| Distribution and analytics | Distribution & Analytics Agent | Track publishing, channel fit, performance notes, repeat/stop decisions |
| Proof review | Proof and Governance Auditor | Check claims, proof, permissions, and hype language before public use |

## 3. Brand System Rules

- [Fact] Preferred language includes strategy, systems, workflows, infrastructure, clarity, brand growth, practical AI, operating pipeline, cinematic content, first-principles thinking, and connected growth system.
- [Fact] Avoid generic AI-agency language such as revolutionary AI, cutting-edge transformation, unlock the future, automate everything, next-gen solutions, and agentic everything.
- [Recommendation] Use visual identity decisions from `03_brand_strategy/positioning/brand_identity_guidelines.md` as the detailed design source.
- [Recommendation] Use this file as the brief-level kit, not as a replacement for the full identity guidelines.

## 4. Approval Boundaries

The Digital Presence Orchestrator may draft, structure, audit, and recommend. It must not directly:

- publish content
- invent proof
- use client logos
- create testimonials or metrics
- change pricing
- make procurement, legal, or financial commitments
- approve public claims without Proof and Governance review

## 6. Default Workflow

1. Founder gives a goal.
2. Digital Presence Orchestrator scopes the work.
3. Orchestrator selects the smallest useful agent team.
4. Specialist agents create drafts, audits, or briefs.
5. Orchestrator assembles one final output.
6. Proof and Governance Auditor reviews public claims.
7. Founder approves public publishing or major public-facing changes.
8. Relevant memory files are updated.

## 7. Quality Gate

Before public-facing output leaves draft status, confirm:

- The work supports Kramaniti's strategy, systems, and content sequence.
- The language is specific to Kramaniti, not generic AI-agency copy.
- Claims are labeled or softened.
- Proof-sensitive material is registered in `09_reviews/proof_register.md`.
- Assets are listed in `08_brand_assets/asset_registry.md` when reused publicly.
- Publishing is explicitly approved by the founder.
