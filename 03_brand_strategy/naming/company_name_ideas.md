# Company Naming Strategy & Evaluation Sheet

This document compiles, evaluates, and ranks premium brand names suitable for Karan Chordia’s AI strategy and content automation studio.

---

## 1. Naming Evaluation Methodology

Each name is scored on a scale of 1 to 5 (with 5 being the highest) across five criteria:
1.  **Sanskrit/Indian Resonance (IR)**: Depth of cultural meaning and local familiarity.
2.  **International Usability (IU)**: Ease of pronunciation, spellcheck ease, and global appeal.
3.  **Premium/B2B Feel (PF)**: Professional authority and suitability for high-ticket enterprise buyers.
4.  **Memorability (MB)**: Brand recall and phonetic strength.
5.  **Domain Risk (DR)**: Estimated availability of premium TLDs (like `.ai`, `.com`, `.studio`) without exorbitant costs (5 = lowest risk, 1 = highest risk).

## 2. The Selected Candidate: Kramaniti (`[NEW]` / `[Fact]` / `[Recommendation]`)

*   **Proposed Name:** Kramaniti (or Krama Niti)
*   **Primary Orientation:** Hybrid (Tech-First Workflow + Narrative Strategy)
*   **Linguistic Roots:**
    1.  **Krama** (Sanskrit: क्रम): Sequence, order, step-by-step progression, systematic process, or workflow (corresponds to algorithmic logic).
    2.  **Niti** (Sanskrit: नीति): Strategy, policy, logic, or ethics.
    3.  **Cultural Layer (Javanese):** Refined etiquette, manners, or "the proper, ethical way of doing things."
*   **Meaning:** **"The Logic of Sequence"**, **"Algorithmic Strategy"**, or **"Systematic Workflows."**
*   **Strategic Rationale:**
    *   *AI Workflows / Agentic Systems:* Directly describes the step-by-step logic (*Krama*) and prompt strategy (*Niti*) of automated LLM agent routing.
    *   *Content Strategy:* Represents creating a systematic process for scaling brand stories and digital presence.
    *   *Ethics / Quality:* The Javanese connotation of "refined conduct" highlights that the studio focuses on high-fidelity, customized AI implementations, rather than generic spam wrappers.
*   **Target Domains:** `kramaniti.com`, `kramaniti.ai`, `kramaniti.studio`
*   **Scores:** IR: 5 | IU: 5 | PF: 5 | MB: 5 | DR: 5 | **Total Score: 5.0 / 5.0**
