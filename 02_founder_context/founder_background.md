## 2. Professional Narrative

### The Genesis: Early Content & Digital Marketing (2016 - 2017)
Karan entered the Bengaluru tech-media ecosystem through digital marketing education at the Web Marketing Academy and freelance photography/videography. In central Bangalore, he established a local freelance brand offering commercial photo and video services, quickly identifying that businesses required more than raw footage—they needed a coherent digital storytelling system.

### B2B Media Ascendancy & WeWork Integration (2017 - 2019)
In August 2017, Karan established his freelance base at WeWork Galaxy (Residency Road, Bangalore) under the brand **HowDramaTech**. He launched a YouTube channel to explain tech issues with a "bit of drama" (storytelling). Over the next three years, he became a core media vendor for the booming co-working ecosystem, producing localized inauguration videos and event coverage for WeWork India properties (Vaishnavi Signature, ITI Limited, RMZ Latitude). He expanded his capabilities to aerial cinematography (drone mapping via DJI Mavic Air) and worked with premium brands like Hyatt Centric and New Horizon Educational Institution. During this era, he recognized the necessity of recurring B2B retainer models over unpredictable freelance contracts.

### The Incubation & Algorithmic Pivot (2020 - 2023)
During the pandemic, public content uploads ceased. This period was not a gap in growth, but rather an intensive, self-directed research and development incubation phase. Karan pivoted from capturing the physical world to analyzing digital systems. He entered the high-frequency cryptocurrency derivative markets, developing a deep understanding of market mechanics, Fibonacci retracements, risk management, and quantitative systems thinking. This phase fostered the technical rigor required to structure complex logic pipelines—a skill that directly translated to the emerging generative AI landscape.

### Strategic Rebirth & AI Tool Building (2023 - Present)
In March 2023, Karan pivoted fully into the AI ecosystem, taking on the role of AI Architect at **Aimpact Space**. He began building custom GPTs (e.g. Coursefy) and sharing insights in the OpenAI developer community regarding knowledge retrieval, JSON formatting, and API optimizations. 
He spent 5 months working with **Nexocean** (a Bengaluru-based workforce consulting company), where he built internal automation tools and produced digital marketing/brand content. 
Today, he operates as a premium solo consultant (via `karan.pop.site`), designing AI playbooks, building bespoke chatbot agents, and architecting automated content engines for founders and scaling startups.
